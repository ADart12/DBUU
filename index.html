About DBUU

Dev Bhoomi Uttarakhand University, recognized by UGC under Section 2(f) of the UGC Act, 1956, is spread across 42 acres of lush greenery. Awarded as the Best Emerging University by the Asian Education Conclave and for Placement & Academic Excellence by the Ministry of HRD, it offers 120+ programs across 11 schools, including Engineering, Management, Law, Pharmacy, Design, Ayurveda, and more. The university is accredited by UGC, PCI, INC, NCISM, CoA, and other statutory bodies.
